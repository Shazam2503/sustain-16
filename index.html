How to Play

  1. Find the four hidden groups. Every puzzle has 16 words that secretly belong to 4 groups of 4. Your job is to figure out which words go together.
  2. Select four words you think belong together. Tap a word to select it. Tap again to deselect. When you've picked four, hit Submit.
  3. Watch the colours reveal themselves. Get it right and the group locks in with a colour showing how tricky it was — green is the easiest, then blue, orange, and red is the trickiest. Get it wrong and you'll lose one of your four mistakes.
  4. Stuck? Use a hint. Tap Hint to reveal one of the hidden group names. You'll still need to figure out which words belong to it.
  5. Solve all four groups before you run out of mistakes. You have four chances. Use them wisely — the red group is always the trickiest, and some words look like they fit in more than one place.
  6. Come back tomorrow. A new puzzle drops every day at midnight, with a brand-new sustainability theme to crack.

Knowledge Nugget 🌿

Ecotourism is one of the fastest-growing segments of the global tourism industry, but the term has no single, universally binding legal definition. The International Ecotourism Society defines genuine ecotourism through 7 principles: covering impact reduction, cultural awareness, conservation funding, community empowerment, and low-impact design. Without these, it's just tourism with a green label.
